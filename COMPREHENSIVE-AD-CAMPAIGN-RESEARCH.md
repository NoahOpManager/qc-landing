### 1.5 Meta Health & Wellness Restrictions ⚠️

**IMPORTANT:** Meta has strict policies on Health & Wellness ads.

**What's Restricted:**
- Ads promoting "medical" benefits
- Claims about treating conditions
- Before/after narratives
- Specific health outcomes

**What's Allowed:**
- General wellness content
- Breathing technique education
- Stress management (general)
- Meditation/mindfulness

**Strategy:**
- Frame ads as "wellness" not "treatment"
- Avoid medical claims
- Use "learn," "discover," "explore" CTAs
- Avoid "cure," "treat," "heal"
