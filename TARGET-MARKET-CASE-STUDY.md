## 🎯 Executive Summary

The Coherent Breathing product targets health-conscious adults interested in breathwork, meditation, and biohacking. The primary audience overlaps with readers of James Nestor's "Breath" and listeners of the Huberman Lab podcast.

## 🔬 Audience Research Sources

### 1. "Breath" by James Nestor (Amazon/Goodreads)
- **1M+ copies sold** globally
- Crossed NY Times bestseller list
- Appeals to: health enthusiasts, alternative medicine seekers, biohackers
- Reader profile: educated, self-improvement oriented, skeptical of mainstream medicine

### 2. Huberman Lab Podcast Data
- Top 10 podcast globally
- **57% male / 43% female**
- **Largest age group: 25-34** (secondary: 35-44)
- Audience: science-literate, health-conscious, early adopters
- Highly engaged — listens to full episodes, buys recommended products

### 3. McKinsey Wellness Report (2025)
- $2T+ global wellness market
- **Younger demographics (Gen Z, Millennials) driving growth**
- But older demographics (45+) increasingly interested
- Focus: sleep, mental health, stress management, mindfulness

### 4. PubMed Clinical Trial (Dec 2023)
- Coherent breathing RCT (N=400) on Prolific platform
- Participants: general population seeking stress resilience tools
- Demographics skew: educated, health-interested, middle-income

## 💰 Ad Creative Recommendations

### Hook Angles (Test These)

1. **"As seen in 'Breath' by James Nestor"** — Leverage existing credibility
2. **"The breathing technique Huberman calls 'essential'"** — Science/authority angle
3. **"5 minutes a day. No app. No device. Just breathe."** — Simplicity angle
4. **"What if better sleep & focus was one breath away?"** — Benefits angle
5. **"HeartMath costs $200+. This technique is free."** — Anti-competitor angle

### Creative Formats
- **Short-form video**: 15-30s breathing visualization (use Coherent Breathing timer videos)
- **Carousel**: Science → Mechanism → CTA
- **Single image**: Before/after stress level, or simple CTA with product shot

### CTA Options
- "Learn the technique →"
- "Get instant access →"
- "Start your practice →"
