## B-Roll Suggestions

1. **Nature footage:** Ocean waves, forest, mountains (calming)
2. **People breathing:** Close-up on chest/diaphragm movement (not face)
3. **Abstract fluid:** Blue water, slow motion liquids (circulation metaphor)
4. **Medical/anatomy:** Diaphragm diagrams (for Science version)
5. **Meditation contexts:** People in peaceful settings

- **ElevenLabs:** When API configured, use warm female or male voice
