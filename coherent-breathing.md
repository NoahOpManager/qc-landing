# Coherent Breathing - Knowledge Base

## About The Method
- **CB = Coherent Breathing** (shorthand for this thread only)
- **Coherent Breathing®** is breathing at 5 breaths per minute (5.5 seconds in, 5.5 seconds out)
- Introduced by **Stephen Elliott** of Coherence LLC in 2005
- The protocol involves breathing at the nominal rate of 5 breaths per minute and relaxation of "The Six Bridges" (6 anatomical zones)
- Benefits: improved brain function via enhanced circulation, autonomic nervous system balance, better HRV
